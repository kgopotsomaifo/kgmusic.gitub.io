A Brief History of AmaPiano:

AmaPiano, a nguni word meaning 'the Piano's is a sub-genre of house music that originated in South Africa, specifically the townships of the country. The sound is a fusion of traditional deep house music, with influences from fields like jazz and another home-made genre, Kwaito which influenced by Hip Hop and Reggae. An important element of the genre is the prevalent use of the "log drum", a wide percussive bassline.The genre's popularity has created a proliferation internationally, and has captivated listeners, especially in Europe and America.
